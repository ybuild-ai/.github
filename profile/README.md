## What is Y Build?

Y Build is the operating system for AI-era entrepreneurs. We help founders turn ideas into real products—without writing code, configuring servers, or hiring developers.

**The problem:** Launching a startup takes months of development, costs tens of thousands of dollars, and requires technical expertise most founders don't have.

**Our solution:** Describe what you want in plain English. AI builds it, deploys it, and helps you grow it.

## Features

### AI Code Generation
Tell AI what you want to build. It writes production-ready code—frontend, backend, database, and APIs. No coding experience required.

### One-Click Deployment
Go live worldwide in seconds. Custom domains, SSL certificates, and global CDN—all configured automatically. No DevOps needed.

### Demo Cut
Your product is ready? AI generates a professional demo video with script, voiceover, and animations. Perfect for Product Hunt launches.

### AI Growth Tools
Built-in SEO optimization, real-time analytics, and marketing automation. Understand your users and grow your business from day one.

### Mobile Dashboard
Manage your business on the go. View analytics, handle orders, and make updates from anywhere.
